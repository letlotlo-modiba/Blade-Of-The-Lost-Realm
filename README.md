🗡️ Blade of the Lost Realm

Blade of the Lost Realm is a story-driven game set in the fractured world of Elarion, where four realms exist in unstable balance. The game blends immersive lore, exploration, and strategic gameplay centered around a legendary artifact — the Eclipsestar.

🌑 Overview

Centuries ago, the Eclipsestar maintained balance between four chaotic realms:

* The Mortal Realm
* The Shadow Realm
* The Astral Realm
* The Beast Realms

After a catastrophic event known as The Sundering, the blade was shattered, and the realms began to collide. Chaos spread, and ancient forces awakened.

You play as a chosen warrior tasked with restoring balance — or reshaping the fate of the realms entirely.

* 🎮 Features (Planned)
* 🌍 Multi-realm exploration system
* ⚔️ Combat mechanics (melee, abilities, progression)
* 🧠 Skill trees and character progression
* 📖 Deep lore and narrative choices
* 🧩 Quest and mission system
* 👾 Enemy AI from different realms
* 🛠️ Configurable game settings (modular design)
